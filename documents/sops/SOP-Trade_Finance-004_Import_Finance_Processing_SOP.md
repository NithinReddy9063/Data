# Import Finance Processing SOP

## 1 Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure defines the controlled operating steps for trade transaction review, document checking, and instrument processing under document SOP-Trade Finance-004. It converts the related policy requirements into repeatable actions for operations, compliance review, escalation, evidence retention, and customer communication.

## 2 Preconditions

| Preconditions | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Registered Request | A customer, account, transaction, facility, or service request has been received through an approved ACB channel. |
| Customer Record | The customer type, product, and risk level are recorded using approved ACB values. |
| Evidence Availability | Mandatory documents or case evidence are attached, requested, or documented as pending. |
| Access Control | The Operations Officer and Operations Manager handling the case are authorized to perform the workflow. |
| Related Policy | The parent policy listed in the References section is applicable to the request. |

## 3 Inputs

| Input | Source | Minimum Quality Standard |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Customer request or case instruction | Relationship Manager, Relationship Officer, customer record, or operations queue | Complete, dated, and linked to the customer record |
| Supporting documents | Customer record or approved document capture channel | Legible, current where required, and aligned to applicable KOD |
| Risk classification | Operations or compliance workflow | Recorded as Low, Medium, High, or Critical |
| Prior decision record | Existing customer or case record where applicable | Retrievable and consistent with current request |

## 4 Procedure

| Step | Role | Action | Evidence Produced |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Operations Officer | Receive the request, confirm the document ID context, and verify that the customer record contains the required product, customer type, and risk level. | Intake note and case reference |
| 2 | Operations Officer | Compare submitted evidence against the related KOD and identify missing, expired, inconsistent, or unclear items. | Evidence review checklist |
| 3 | Operations Officer | Record the preliminary decision and route standard cases for completion or non-standard cases to the Operations Manager. | Preliminary decision note |
| 4 | Operations Manager | Review exceptions, SLA concerns, and discrepancies using the related Exception Matrix. | Supervisory review outcome |
| 5 | Compliance Officer | Provide compliance interpretation when policy application, customer risk, or evidence sufficiency is unclear. | Compliance review note |
| 6 | Senior Compliance Officer | Review High risk cases and determine whether enhanced review or escalation is required. | Enhanced review decision |
| 7 | Operations Manager | Confirm final operating decision, required customer communication, and record retention action. | Final operations decision |
| 8 | Operations Officer | Update the customer or case record and close the workflow only when mandatory evidence and approvals are complete. | Closure note and retained evidence |

## 5 Decision Points

| Decision Point | Proceed When | Escalate When | Approval Required |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Evidence Completeness | All mandatory evidence is present and linked to the customer record | Evidence is missing, expired, or illegible | Operations Manager |
| Data Consistency | Customer, product, and request data are consistent across records | Material discrepancy affects identity, authority, eligibility, or transaction purpose | Senior Compliance Officer |
| Risk Classification | Risk level is Low or Medium and no enhanced review indicator exists | Risk level is High or Critical | Senior Compliance Officer or Chief Compliance Officer |
| SLA Control | The workflow can be completed within Trade Transaction Review - 1 Business Day | SLA breach is likely or has occurred | Branch Manager |

## 6 Outputs

| Output | Required Content | Record Location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Completed review decision | Accepted, rejected, remediated, or escalated decision with rationale | Customer record or case record |
| Exception record | Scenario, risk level, approval, SLA impact, and related EXM reference | Exception record linked to customer or case |
| Customer communication | Clear request for missing evidence or decision outcome where applicable | Customer interaction record |
| Audit evidence | Checklist, supporting documents, approvals, and closure note | Controlled document repository or workflow record |

## 7 Exception Handling

| Exception | Handling Requirement | Approval |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Missing evidence | Request remediation and suspend completion until evidence is received or approved exception is recorded | Operations Manager |
| Material discrepancy | Escalate for enhanced review and do not complete workflow until decision is recorded | Senior Compliance Officer |
| Critical risk condition | Hold processing and escalate through the approved hierarchy | Chief Compliance Officer |
| Customer communication delay | Record reason, expected completion date, and customer impact | Branch Manager |

## 8 Escalation

| Escalation Level | Trigger | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Operations Officer | First-level review identifies a blocker | Record blocker and route to Operations Manager |
| Operations Manager | Exception, discrepancy, or SLA risk requires supervisory decision | Approve remediation or escalate |
| Branch Manager | Customer-facing impact or unresolved branch issue exists | Confirm operating position and customer handling |
| Regional Manager | Repeated branch issue or regional inconsistency is identified | Review consistency and route material matters |
| Department Head | Systemic control concern or procedure interpretation is required | Determine corrective action |
| Compliance Head | Material compliance concern requires senior compliance decision | Confirm compliance position |
| Chief Compliance Officer | Critical risk approval is required | Record final decision |

## 9 KPIs

| KPI | Target | Measurement Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SLA adherence | 100 percent of cases completed or escalated within approved SLA | Workflow timestamp report |
| Evidence completeness | 100 percent of completed cases contain mandatory evidence | Quality review sample |
| Exception documentation | 100 percent of exceptions include approval and rationale | Exception register |
| Audit retrievability | 100 percent of sampled records are retrievable | Quarterly control sample |
